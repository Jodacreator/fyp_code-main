Instructions

You will take part in a short decision-making activity with {{ n }} players. The activity lasts for a fixed number of rounds.

What happens in each round
  • You will see some HIGH / LOW signals on your screen.
  • These signals are shown automatically by the system.
  • You will then choose how much to contribute to a shared financial project.
Whose signals you see
    {% if network_type == 'ring' %}
  • You see your own signal.
  • You also see the signals of two other players connected to you.
    • {% else %}
  • You see your own signal.
  • One player is randomly selected at the start to be the Hub, and stays the Hub for all rounds.
  • If you are not the Hub, you also see the Hub’s signal.
  • If you are the Hub, you see everyone’s signals.
    • {% endif %}
How earnings are calculated
  • Each round you receive an endowment of {{ endowment }}.
  • You choose a contribution (0–20). Your payoff is:
Payoff = (Endowment − Your Contribution) + MPCR × (Total Group Contribution)
  • MPCR is fixed for the entire session but can be either HIGH or a LOW value.
  • The HIGH/LOW state is chosen at the start and stays the same across all rounds.
Your decisions
  • Contribution: choose how much to contribute (from {{ min_contrib }} to {{ max_contrib }}).
  • Belief report: use the slider (0–100) to indicate how likely you think the true state is HIGH. 0 means you are certain the state is LOW, and 100 means you are certain it is HIGH.
Your belief report does not affect your earnings and is not shown to other players.

You will see your result at the end of each round.
